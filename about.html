About Us

Real People.
Right Here in Weaverville.

Peterson Insurance Group is a family-owned independent insurance agency on Main Street in Weaverville, NC. We've been here since 2001, and we're not going anywhere.

We're not a call center. We're not a website with a chatbot. We're the office with flowers by the door and artwork on the walls. When you call, a real person picks up. When you walk in, someone knows your name.

Our Story

A Family Business, Built on Trust

Stom Peterson spent over a decade working in the insurance industry before deciding to open his own agency in 2001. He wanted something different — an agency that was small enough to know every client by name but experienced enough to handle anything that came through the door.

He brought his daughter Eirene on board from the start. She's been managing operations, writing policies, and building relationships with clients ever since. Today, Eirene runs the agency as the principal agent — certified by Erie Insurance, trained in life, commercial, and Medicare Supplement lines, and deeply connected to the community she serves.

Stom specializes in commercial and life insurance and still works with longtime clients. He's transitioning toward semi-retirement — camping with his wife, traveling in his motor home, and volunteering in the community — but he's still the one people call when they need someone who's seen it all.

Peterson Insurance is a woman-owned business. Eirene leads a team of five, and together they handle over 200 calls a day from an office that feels more like a living room than a corporate suite. The artwork on the walls? That's Eirene's — she's a painter and potter whose work is carried at Reems Creek Pottery, right here in Weaverville.

That's the kind of agency this is. Real people, with real lives, doing real work for their neighbors.

When It Mattered Most

Hurricane Helene

When Hurricane Helene hit Western North Carolina, a lot of things shut down. Some agencies went dark for weeks. We didn't.

Peterson Insurance set up a temporary office, worked around the clock, and made sure every client could reach us. We processed claims, answered questions, and helped people navigate one of the most difficult periods this community has ever faced.

That's what being local means. It's not a marketing line — it's a commitment. We live here. We work here. And when our neighbors needed us, we showed up.

Our Community

Part of Weaverville, Not Just Located Here
