Need Help with Your Policy?

Whether you need to file a claim, make a payment, request a certificate, or just have a question about your coverage — we're here. You don't have to navigate this alone.

The fastest way to get help is to call us at 828-645-8777 or email info@petersoninsurance.group. We handle claims, policy changes, certificates, and billing questions. We try to return all calls the same business day. To make sure we can give you our full attention, we recommend scheduling an appointment — call or email and we'll find a time that works. Walk-ins are always welcome, but an appointment helps reduce any wait time.
