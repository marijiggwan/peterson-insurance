What We Cover

Commercial Coverage for Every Stage of Business

We represent multiple carriers for commercial lines — including Erie Insurance, Progressive, and National General — so we can find the right fit for your industry, your size, and your budget.

General Liability

The foundation of any business insurance plan. Covers third-party bodily injury, property damage, and personal injury claims — the things that can happen to anyone, anytime.

Customer injuries on your premises
Damage caused by your work or products
Advertising injury and legal defense costs
Available through Erie Insurance, Progressive, and other carriers

Commercial Auto

If your business uses vehicles — trucks, vans, work cars, or a fleet — commercial auto covers liability, collision, and comprehensive for vehicles used on the job.

Company-owned vehicles and fleets
Employee-driven vehicles for work
Hired and non-owned auto coverage
Available through Erie Insurance, Progressive, National General

Workers Compensation

Required in NC for businesses with three or more employees. Covers medical expenses and lost wages when an employee is injured on the job. Protects your team and your business.

Medical bills for workplace injuries
Lost wages during recovery
Employer liability protection
Required in NC for 3+ employees

Commercial Property

Covers the physical stuff — your building (if you own it), equipment, inventory, furniture, and anything else your business needs to operate.

Building and contents coverage
Equipment and inventory protection
Business interruption coverage
Available through Erie Insurance and other carriers

Bonds

Some businesses and contractors need surety bonds to operate or bid on projects. We can help you get bonded — whether it's a contractor bond, permit bond, or performance bond.

Contractor and surety bonds
Permit and license bonds
Performance and bid bonds
Talk to us about your bonding needs

Professional Liability

Also known as errors and omissions (E&O), this covers claims that your professional services or advice caused a client financial harm. Important for consultants, accountants, and service providers.

Professional errors and omissions
Legal defense costs
Negligence and misrepresentation claims
Available for various industries
Smart Bundling

Business Owner's Policy (BOP)

If you're a small to mid-size business, a BOP is often the smartest way to get covered. It bundles your general liability and commercial property into one policy — usually at a lower cost than buying each separately.

BOPs can also be customized with add-ons depending on your industry:

  • Business interruption coverage
  • Data breach and cyber liability
  • Equipment breakdown
  • Hired and non-owned auto
  • Employee dishonesty
