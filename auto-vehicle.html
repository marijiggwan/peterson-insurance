Know Your Coverage

North Carolina Auto Insurance Requirements

NC law requires all registered vehicles to carry minimum liability coverage. But the state minimums are just that — minimums. They may not be enough to protect you in a serious accident.

We always walk through your specific situation and recommend coverage levels that actually make sense for your vehicle, your assets, and your family. No upselling — just making sure you're not underinsured.

NC Minimum Requirements

Bodily Injury (per person)$30,000
Bodily Injury (per accident)$60,000
Property Damage$25,000

Underinsured MotoristRequired

Classic & Collector

Your Classic Deserves More Than Standard Coverage

Standard auto insurance depreciates your vehicle's value. For a classic car, truck, or collector vehicle, that's the wrong approach entirely. Through Hagerty, we provide agreed-value coverage — meaning you and the insurer agree on what the vehicle is worth, and that's what you're covered for.
