Who We Are

Your Neighbors in Insurance Since 2001

Peterson Insurance Group was founded by Stom Peterson right here in Weaverville. Today, his daughter Eirene Peterson carries on the family tradition — running an agency where people walk in, sit down, and get real help from someone who knows their name.

We're an independent agency, which means we represent you — not one insurance company. We work with multiple carriers to find coverage that fits your life. But what makes us different isn't just the options. It's that we're here. Same office. Same hours. Same people. When something happens, you don't call a number and wait on hold. You call us, or you walk in.

When Hurricane Helene hit our community, we didn't disappear. We set up a temporary office, worked around the clock, and made sure every client could reach us. That's what being local means — we're here when it matters most.
